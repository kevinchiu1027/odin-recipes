Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipes

Description

What makes this chocolate chip cookie recipe best? The addition of browned butter really puts these cookies over the top. It makes them crispy-edged, chewy-centered, yet still rooted in classic cookie flavor. Oh, and no mixer required, so thereâ€™s no excuse not to make them.

Ingredients

  • all purpose flower
  • kosher salt
  • baking soda
  • unsalted butter
  • dark brown sugar
  • granulated sugar
  • large egg
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • vanilla extract
  • bittersweet chocolate

Steps

  1. prheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. whisk flour, salt, and baking powder; set aside
  3. cook butter in large saucepan over medium heat, remove when it browns (~4 minute)
  4. add sugars to butter
  5. add eggs and egg yolks to butter and sugars
  6. add in vanilla extract
  7. fold in dry ingredients into wet ingredients
  8. fold in chocolate
  9. let dough rest 5-10 minutes
  10. scoop out 1.5 oz dough balls and line them on baking sheet
  11. bake until deep golden brown (8-10 minutes)
  12. rest on baking sheet
